# 💫 AnKang - The elite productivity suite for Anki
<img width="2000" height="652" alt="logo" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/662f8f3f-8bb8-4b21-bb8d-51f2441814c7" />

AnKang is a high-performance framework for Anki designed specifically for medical students. It transforms the standard review interface into a modular "Command Center," integrating real-time AI assistance and productivity widgets directly into your workflow.

## ✨ Features

### 🛠️ The "Hub" Sidebar (Left)
A sleek navigation hub designed to keep your most essential widgets a click away without distracting from your cards.
* **To-Do List Widget:** Keep track of your daily Anki deck targets.
* **Notes:** Have a built-in notes app that can even have links to individual cards you want saved separately.
* **Exam Countdown:** Always keep track of how many days away your exam is, with up to three exams trackable at once.
* **Timer Widget:** Set timers for yourself to keep your studying efficient, or utilize it as a Pomodoro Timer.
* **Stopwatch Widget:** Keep track of how long you've been studying for those longer, lazier days, or time yourself on individual decks.

### 🤖 The AI/Web Assistant Sidebar (Right)
A FREE AI sidekick that lives in your right sidebar, ready to explain complex medical concepts instantly.
* **Contextual AI:** Select text on any card to send it directly to your assistant or to a Google Search.
* **Quick Prompts:** With a click of a button, ask AI to explain, simplify, or test you on the current Anki Card.

### 🚫 Minimize Sidebars
Need some space and don't want any of these features distracting you or taking up screen space?
* With a click of a button, minimize the Left Sidebar, Right Sidebar, or both! Keep things out of the way so you can focus on your studies.

## 🚀 Installation

1.  Open Anki.
2.  Go to `Tools` -> `Add-ons`.
3.  Click `Get Add-ons...`, and enter the AnkiWeb code: **1680917863**, OR
4.  Click `Install from file...`, and install from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/dr-gkang/AnKang/releases)
5.  Restart Anki.
